Let's start

1.Choose a task that you want to get done.

2.Set the tomato for 25 minutes(or more or less if you want).

3.Work on the task until the tomato rings.

4.Make a tick on a paper.

5.Take 5 minute of break (you deserve it) until the tomato rings again.

6.After the fourth tomato, take a long break.
